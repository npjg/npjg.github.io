Trivia

Hello, fellow surfer! I’m a mathematics major at Liberty University. I also delude myself into thinking that I have time for my minors in philosophy, computer science, and piano performance.

I was born and raised in Hawaii, and I now live in the eastern United States. When I was ten, I first started programming with QBasic. Quickly thereafter, I switched to Visual Basic and C. After a long hiatus for music and competitive forensics, I learned C++ at university and began the arduous trek toward Emacs mastery.

When I’m not hacking away on my beloved ThinkPad 450s, I can probably be found engrossed in Chopin or studying a Vaughan Williams score.
